Privacy Policy for MusicVapey

1. Information We Collect

When you authorize MusicVapey to access your TikTok account, we collect your public profile information (such as username and avatar) and public video data (such as captions, hashtags, and sound IDs). We do not collect your private messages, email address, password, or watch history.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your public TikTok content solely to analyze themes, moods, and interests in order to generate personalized music playlists. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or resale to third parties.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored temporarily and processed only during your session. We do not retain your TikTok data beyond the immediate use for generating playlist results. We follow reasonable security practices to protect any temporary session data.

4. Third-Party Services

MusicVapey may use APIs from TikTok and Spotify to access video and music information. These services have their own privacy policies which govern their data handling practices.

5. Your Rights

You can revoke access to your TikTok account at any time via TikTok's account settings. If you wish to delete any data you shared with us during your session, please contact us directly.

6. Contact

For privacy-related questions or data deletion requests, please contact: support@musicvapey.com
